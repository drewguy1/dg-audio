About Me

Drew Guy

POST SOUND ENGINEER - COMPOSER - WEB DESIGNER

To most people, sound in film and television is just a last small piece to the puzzle, but for Drew it's much more than that. Drew has always had a love for sound. It started out at age 10 when he first picked up drum sticks and just wanted to sound better. After a vast and impressive performance career as a percussionist that spans over a decade, Drew began teaching some of the world's most talented and widely acclaimed percussion ensembles with only one goal in mind... To make them sound better. Through those experiences Drew has developed a keen sense of quality audio and, with a unique and aggressive approach, has dedicated it as a career path all in an effort to make his works sound better. Drew is an awarding winning sound editor, mixer and film composer. As a sound editor and re-recording mixer he has worked on podcasts, film scores, Emmy winning television shows, Academy Award Nominated and Academy Award Winning films. As a composer, Drew has written and arranged music for high school marching bands, indoor percussion ensembles, Drum Corps International, Winter Guard International, and a variety of film and television projects. Most notibly, he was the composer for a film titled "Going Up". The film recieved heavy praise from many of the country's most well known film festivals and at a few Drew recieved the award of "Best Original Score".
